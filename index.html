I am a front-end web developer living in Philadelphia, PA

Thanks for visiting. My name is LeeAnn and I like to create websites that are user friendly and implement the latest web standards. I am a web accessibility advocate trying to spread the word and teach others why making accessible websites is so important. I am a teacher, TA and events coordinator for Girl Develop It Philadelphia,   organizer of LadyHacks  and the Philly Women in Tech Summit. Sometimes I can be found blogging at leekinney.is,   SheTechPHL   and    Philly Hikes. In my spare time I love to hike, bike, camp and travel as much as possible.
